Types of Loans

What types of loans are available for
businesses with bad credit?

Businesses with a lower credit score can still access funding. Here are some common options your business could consider.

Unsecured Business Loans

Funding without requiring business assets as security, often suited to fast access needs.

Secured Business Loans

Larger loan amounts can be available when borrowing is secured against business assets.

Merchant Cash Advance

Access funding based on card takings, with repayments linked to future sales volume.

Asset Finance

Spread the cost of equipment or vehicles while preserving working capital for operations.

Invoice Finance

Unlock cash tied up in unpaid invoices to keep cash flow healthy and predictable.

Guarantor Business Loans

A third-party guarantor may support access to funding when lenders need extra reassurance.
